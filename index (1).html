About Me

Larisa Avetisyan is a Sophomore student at Marlboro High School. She enjoys reading, spending time with family and friends, and making art. After high school, Larisa plans to go to college to study in the medical or scientific field. Though coding is not directly involved in these fields, she is currently in a web design class in hopes of learning how to successfully create her own websites. Seeing how much the world relies on the internet, she aspires to learn how to code as it will definitely be of use in school and later in the workplace. Larisa believes that there is always room for improvement. She is always looking for something new to learn, to expand her knowledge into other areas.
